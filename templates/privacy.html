Privacy Policy

This application, Aim for the Stars, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

What Data Is Collected?

  • Email address and display name (for registration and login)
  • Diary entries, goals, and progress statistics you create
  • Session cookies (for login and security)

How Is Your Data Used?

  • To provide and secure your account
  • To enable diary, goal, and progress features
  • To maintain your session while logged in

Cookies

Only essential cookies are used for login and session management. No tracking or analytics cookies are used.

Your Rights

  • You can download your data at any time from your Profile page.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all data (feature coming soon).
  • You can contact the developer for any privacy concerns.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer via the project repository.
