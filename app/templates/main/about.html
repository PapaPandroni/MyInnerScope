About My Inner Scope

My Inner Scope is a personal journey towards self-reflection and continuous growth. Born from the insights gained through years of personal development and therapy, this platform is designed to empower you to take control of your inner world and gain profound insights into your own actions and behaviors.

We believe that true self-improvement begins with understanding. Research shows that self-reflection - the act of reflecting on your daily actions, categorizing them, and considering their impact, whether "good" or "bad", is half the battle. It's about knowing your problems, acknowledging your patterns, and consciously working towards a better you.

A core philosophy embedded within My Inner Scope is the concept of "Värderad Riktning" (Valued Direction), a powerful idea from Acceptance and Commitment Therapy. This isn't about setting an achievable goal with a finish line, but rather an ongoing, aspirational direction, like "being a better father" or "living more kindly." There's no end to such a goal, but every action you take either moves you closer to that valued direction or further away. This continuous evaluation is the key thought process we aim to foster within you.

My Inner Scope provides the tools to track your reflections, set meaningful goals, and visualize your progress through gamified elements like points and streaks. It's a supportive environment to help you consistently engage with your self-improvement journey.

This application was created by Peremil Starklint Söderström, with the hope of sharing effective strategies for personal growth that have proven invaluable on his own path.
