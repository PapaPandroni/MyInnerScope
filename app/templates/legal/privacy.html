Privacy Policy

This application, My Inner Scope, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

What Data Is Collected?

Essential Data (Required for service):

  • Email address and display name (for registration and login)
  • Diary entries, goals, and progress statistics you create
  • Session cookies (for login and security)

Analytics Data (Optional, with your consent):

  • Page views and navigation patterns (anonymized)
  • Feature usage and interaction data
  • Device and browser information (anonymized)
  • Session duration and frequency of visits
  • IP addresses (anonymized for privacy protection)

How Is Your Data Used?

Essential Data:

  • To provide and secure your account
  • To enable diary, goal, and progress features
  • To maintain your session while logged in

Analytics Data (only if you consent):

  • To understand how users interact with the application
  • To identify popular features and areas for improvement
  • To analyze user behavior patterns (anonymized)
  • To improve application performance and user experience

Cookies

Essential Cookies: Required cookies for login, security, and session management. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Optional): With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how you use the app and improve it. These cookies:

  • Collect anonymized usage data (IP addresses are anonymized)
  • Track page views, feature usage, and user interactions
  • Help us improve the application based on user behavior
  • Can be disabled at any time through cookie preferences
  • Are only activated if you explicitly consent

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using our cookie consent banner.

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to Access: You can download your complete data archive (including diary entries, goals, and stats) at any time from your Profile page in JSON or CSV format.
  • Right to Erasure: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data. This action is irreversible and will remove all your personal information from our systems.
  • Right to Rectification: You can manage your display name and other profile settings directly on your Profile page.
  • Right to Object: You may contact the developer to object to data processing, although data processing is limited to what is essential for application functionality.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer via the project repository.
